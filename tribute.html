Timeline of Milk's Life

May 22nd, 1930 - Harvey Milk is born in Woodmere, New York.

1943-1947 - Milk attends Bay Shore High School as a basketball player and a football captain. He knows he is gay but is still in the closet at this time.

1947-1951 - Harvey attends New York State Teachers College in Albany and earns a math degree.

1951-1955 - Harvey serves in the Navy and is an expert deep sea diver. He is honorably discharged.

1956-1961 - Milk meets Joe Campbell. They will be together for 6 years. At this time, Milk is a high school math teacher and a basketball coach at Hewlitt High school in NYC.

1970 - After almost a decade of living in the closet, Milk finally has the courage to open his sexuality.

1972 - Harvey and his new boyfriend, Scott Smith, move to the Castro in San Francisco.

November, 1973 - Milk runs for supervisor of San Francisco for the first time. He does not have enough support and he loses by a landslide.

November 8th, 1977 - Milk is elected to the board of supervisors for his district in his fourth run for elected office. He becomes the first openly gay man elected to public office in the US.

November, 1978 - Harvey and his protests defeated a new bill which would have fired all gay teachers and their supporters. One of Milk's greatest accomplishments.

November 27th, 1978 - Dan White assassinates Harvey Milk and Mayor George Moscone at City Hall. Over 30,000 people peacefully march from Castro to City Hall and hold a candlelight vigil.

December 2nd, 1978 - Milk's friends scatter his ashes, along with Kool-Aid and bubble bath, into the pacific ocean.

May 21st, 1979 - Dan White is sentenced to only 7 years in prison. Thousands of people are furious and hold riots at City Hall. These riots were known as the White Night Riots.

January 7th, 1984 - Dan White is released from prison after only 5 years and moves back to San Francisco with his family.

October 21st, 1985 - Dan White commits suicide in his garage.

This timeline is courtesy of Lauren Beck for her excellent research and hard work.
