ABOUT ME

I'm 22 years old and I started college in 2020. I like planning games and making them on Construct 3. Also, I've been studying voice acting since I was 13 (ALMOST A V.A. NOW \o/).
I'm more used to editing audio related stuff, like recreating all the background sound effects of movies/series so people can dub it to practice dubbing.
I love planning games and making them on Construct 3 and editing the sounds and creating buttons and menus on Photoshop. I LOVE COOL VISUAL THINGS *u*
I started with game development 'cause it's creative and I had some experience with audio editing. Some months ago I started studying HTML and CSS and I LOVED IT (like I said "VISUAL THINGS").
And on top of everything, my biggest plan is to move to Canada. I have English C2 level (CERTIFICATE HERE). I will try to be a voice actor in Canada too, and I could be one in Brazil at the same time since now many studios are recording remotely.

TL;DR: I like voice acting, making games, editing in Photoshop and making websites with HTML and CSS, but I'm still learning Javascript.
